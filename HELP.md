## EV Charging Tariff Calculator

### Introduction
The EV Charging Tariff Calculator is an application designed to help users analyze and optimize their electric vehicle charging costs. It provides insights into tariffs, breakeven points, and troubleshooting tips for users to make informed decisions about their EV charging.

### Nutzungshinweis (Usage Guide)
1. **Start the Application:** Launch the EV Charging Tariff Calculator application.
2. **Input Data:** Enter parameters such as charging time, cost per kWh, and vehicle specifications.
3. **Analyze:** View the results including costs, tariffs, and suggested charging times.

### Tariff Explanation
- **Tarifdetails (Tariff Details):** The calculator provides various tariff options, allowing users to compare costs based on their charging habits.
- **Billige Tarife (Cheap Tariffs):** Recommendations for low-cost charging periods are provided.

### Breakeven Analysis
- **Breakeven-Punkt (Breakeven Point):** The application calculates when your EV charging investment will pay off based on current electricity prices and usage.

### Troubleshooting
- **Common Issues:** If the application does not start, check for updates or reinstall.
- **Data Entry Errors:** Ensure all fields are filled correctly and review input data before recalculating.

### Abschluss (Conclusion)
The EV Charging Tariff Calculator helps users better understand their electric vehicle charging costs, optimizing for financial savings. For further support, refer to the troubleshooting section or contact our helpdesk.
